ABOUT

A Small Workshop
With Big Ideas

PlasticBlocks3D is a creative hobby focused on designing custom parts, solving interesting problems, and building one-of-a-kind projects.

My Story

PlasticBlocks3D began as a hobby and quickly became one of my favorite creative outlets. I enjoy taking ideas that exist only on paper—or even just in someone's imagination—and turning them into real, physical objects.

Whether it's designing a custom part, solving a unique problem, or creating something completely original, every project gives me the opportunity to learn something new and build something meaningful.

What I Enjoy Building

  • 🛠 Custom CAD designs
  • 🚂 LEGO-compatible projects
  • 📷 Photography accessories
  • 🎭 Wearable designs and prototypes
  • 💡 One-of-a-kind custom creations

My Approach

I enjoy working directly with people, understanding what they're hoping to create, and building something that solves a problem or brings an idea to life. Because this is a hobby workshop, every project receives personal attention from start to finish.
