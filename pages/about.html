About PlasticBlocks3D

Built by a maker who enjoys turning ideas into real objects.

My Story

PlasticBlocks3D started with a simple idea: create useful, creative, and custom-designed objects using modern 3D printing technology.

From replacement parts to completely original creations, every project is approached with attention to detail and a focus on making something that works.

What I Do

I specialize in:

  • Custom CAD design
  • 3D printing
  • Replacement parts
  • Prototype development
  • Creative one-of-a-kind projects

My Approach

Every project begins with understanding the goal, designing a solution, and refining it until it is ready to become a physical object.

Whether it is a small repair part or a completely new idea, I enjoy the challenge of bringing concepts into reality.
